Device Certification

The best devices we certify for BBC products pass our certification process in a single cycle of testing. They do so based on the key criteria outlined below.

Our Criteria

 

Performance

Fast app launch

We expect devices to be able to launch our apps within 10 seconds of a keypress regardless of the launch point.

Responsive UI & Navigation

It is important that devices allow smooth, crisp and snappy transitions through the UI and personalised menus.

Playback

Zero buffering & Fast ABR

We have taken the time to make a stream available to our users regardless of network speeds; devices must be able to retrieve the best streams with zero buffering and in the shortest time.

UHD/HLG (DASH)

Our premium content will be arriving over the coming months; to be able to play that content our devices need to have full support for HLG and be DASH enabled.

Discovery

High prominence

Our apps should be prominently positioned, accessible with just a few clicks and displayed with high resolution logos and images for discoverable content.

Broadcast discovery

HbbTV is coming and we expect it to enable a seamless experience from broadcast to IP with rich follow on experiences.

Deeplinking - EPG & TV UI

Easy app access is key for ous users; Portals, EPG’s and TV UI’s are premium places for our users to discover and directly launch our applications to enjoy rich BBC content.

Live restart from anywhere

Whereever our users are watching live content, we expect there to be a link for our users to restart that content via our players.
